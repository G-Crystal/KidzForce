About Us

KIDZFORCE is a service designed to connect neighbors and local children to projects and chores inside their communities while providing a level of safety by verifying identity and monitoring of dubious activities from the project poster.

Our mobile application is designed to work directly with our website creating a seamless experience for the user and allows parents and guardians to keep a watchful eye on their children's activities while they earn some real cash and collect points with Kidz Force.

For more information about us visit our website at www.kidzforce.com.
